# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Startup community contacts, recruiters, founders, operators, and investors who have met Adolfo or heard about him and want a fast, credible read on who he is, what kind of technical work he has led, and where to reach him.

## Product Purpose

This personal site is Adolfo's durable stamp on the internet. It should communicate identity, current experience, technical leadership, and contact paths in one polished single-scroll page, while keeping the private AI portfolio clearly gated.

## Brand Personality

Precise, technical, understated. The site should feel calm and intentional, with enough visual taste to signal craft without becoming a portfolio performance.

## Anti-references

Avoid SaaS landing-page tropes, oversized decorative cards, brittle remote logo assets, generic dashboard styling, dense CV layouts, and loud product marketing. The site should not feel like a startup homepage or a template resume.

## Design Principles

- First-glance clarity: answer who Adolfo is and where to find him within seconds.
- Taste through restraint: use spacing, typography, and alignment as the primary design signal.
- Credibility over decoration: every company, link, and protected boundary must work cleanly.
- One-page confidence: keep the public experience scannable in a single scroll rather than fragmenting into pages.
- Private means private: authenticated work should feel intentionally gated, not accidentally hidden.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion-safe interactions, and responsive layouts with no horizontal overflow on mobile.
